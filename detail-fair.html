มหกรรมการเงินครั้งที่ 18

The 32nd ASEAN’s Largest Building Technology Exposition

วันที่จัดงาน

1 - 10 ธ.ค. 2560

เวลาจัดงาน

10.00 - 21.00

สถานที่จัดงาน

Bitec Bangna Bangkok

Architect Expo has been organized annually to promote building materials technologies. For the event in 2018, the expo will be the 32nd year in succession of its kind.

75,000 square meters area will be transformed into the marketplace of products and services in construction and building industries. This platform is where supply meets demand for both visitors and the exhibiting companies, with 850 worldwide exhibitors and 400,000 quality visitors in 2017. Manufacturers present their company and products to the whole community and observe and analyse the market and position themselves for the competition at the same time.
